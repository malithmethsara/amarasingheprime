Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I calculate the import tax for a vehicle in Sri Lanka?

The total import tax includes CID, Surcharge, Excise Duty, Luxury Tax, VEL, and VAT. Our calculator uses 2026 Gazette rates for accurate estimates.

2. What is the CIF Value and why is it important?

CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight) is the vehicle's total value upon arrival in Sri Lanka. Accurate CIF values ensure precise tax estimates.

3. Are hybrid vehicles taxed differently?

Yes, hybrids have different excise duty rates to promote eco-friendly vehicles. Our calculator reflects these rates automatically.
